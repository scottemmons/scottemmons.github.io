I am motivated by...

Service

I am motivated by serving others. Seeing the light of understanding flash in a student’s eye kept me writing lesson plans as I taught math in the Mississippi Delta; watching the look of wonder as a client visited her new website kept me developing websites through Sparq Creative Solutions, LLC.

Challenge

I am motivated by challenge. It was exhilarating to walk into the Cyberinfrastructure for Network Science Center of Indiana University, ask a research question that no one had yet answered, and research tirelessly to find a solution.

The Big Picture

I am motivated by big pictures. No Information Visualization MOOC student’s question was too rudimentary to answer remembering that I was providing graduate-level education for free to students from around the globe; no forgotten semicolon was too frustrating to debug remembering that I was pushing the frontiers of network science.
