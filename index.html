Scott Emmons

I am a third-year undergraduate student studying math and computer science at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and Duke University through the Robertson Scholars Leadership Program. I am interested in applied math and computer science research. I have experience in robotics, network science, and information visualization.

In high school, I began working with Katy Börner and the Cyberinfrastructure for Network Science Center of Indiana University in collaboration with Stephen Kobourov of the University of Arizona. In college, I have worked with Kris Hauser in the Intelligent Motion Laboratory, Peter Mucha in the Carolina Center for Interdisciplinary Applied Mathematics, and Carol Arnosti in the Arnosti Lab.
